Collectors

Mixi Collectors is our annual acquisition program for artists. Each edition selects a small group of artists through open call, convenes a specialized jury, and awards the winning work a place in Mixi's permanent collection.

Currently closed · Next edition TBA
Who can apply
01 Artists at any career stage — no minimum exhibition history required.
02 Each edition focuses on a specific theme or medium. Ed. 1 focused on chromatic possibilities in painting.
03 Applications are reviewed by a jury of specialists from galleries, collections, and curatorial practices.
04 The winning work is acquired into Mixi's private collection — a permanent record of the program's history.
