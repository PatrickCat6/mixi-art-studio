About the Studio

A transdisciplinary
laboratory with
Latin roots

Founded in 2022 in Salt Lake City, Mixi Art Studio is a space of convergence for artists who cross borders between disciplines, geographies, and languages. We connect the global art scene bridging Latin American and North American contemporary practices.
